Battle Eternal is a dystopian story cast in a near-future Earth with a tyrannical one-world government, working with the Vatican, that is then reshaped by the Quantum Fracture. As gods incarnate, powers awaken, and tyrants tighten their grip, humanity's revolt ignites‚Äîthe Age of Chaos.

Players step into a universe where legendary creatures return, ancient prophecies awaken, and the very fabric of reality bends to those bold enough to wield it. At its heart stand Alexander, Crystalis, Castor, and DeMarco - Earthly representatives of Ancient Gods whose choices will determine whether humanity ascends to consciousness sovereignty, or falls into eternal digital enslavement.

These are the chronicles of a war to restore reality and sovereignty, where myth collides with machine.
