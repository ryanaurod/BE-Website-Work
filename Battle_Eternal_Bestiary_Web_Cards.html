His story appears in various forms from Celtic hero tales to Germanic warrior epics, showing the universal appeal of the tragic invincible hero. His divinely-forged armor and weapons could pierce any defense, his speed in battle was supernatural, and his battle-fury could inspire terror in entire armies, though his famous heel remained his fatal weakness.
